How to Play

Welcome to Hangman!

1. Play

2. Pick level: Easy (15 lives) | Medium (12)| Hard (9).

3. Tap letters to guess:

Correct = points

Wrong = lose lives & points

Ends when word is solved or lives run out.

Two Player Mode

Player 1 > Score > Player 2's Turn > Next for scoreboard.
